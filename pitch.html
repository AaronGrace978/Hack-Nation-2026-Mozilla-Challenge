The Solution

A portable AI companion
for every website.

Nexus lives in your browser sidebar. An Orchestrator turns your words into a plan and coordinates specialist agents. It reads pages, clicks buttons, compares prices across sites, remembers your preferences — all with explicit, scoped, time-bounded permissions.

03 / 11
How It Works

Four steps. That's it.

1

You talk to Nexus

Type or speak a request in the sidebar. "Add these AirPods to my cart" or "Find the cheapest RTX 5080."

2

The Orchestrator breaks it down

Your request is sent to an LLM that figures out what needs to happen and which agents should do it. No code, no selectors — just plain language in, structured plan out.

3

Specialist agents do the work

The Navigator reads pages and clicks buttons. The Researcher searches multiple sites in parallel. The Memory agent remembers what you like. The Guardian asks permission before anything risky.

4

You get a clear answer

Results are merged and presented in the sidebar. Every action the AI took is logged in the Activity tab — nothing happens behind your back.

Key Differentiator

Permission-First Design

Every action requires explicit, scoped, time-bounded permission.
The user is always in control.

🟢 READ_ONLY 🔵 NAVIGATE 🟠 INTERACT 🟣 SUBMIT 🔴 PURCHASE
✕ Auto-submit payments
✕ Access passwords or credentials
✕ Modify security settings

Full audit trail. Every action logged and inspectable.
